Lifts

11 lifts to keep you going.

  • ▶︎ Ariel Tram: From Snowbird center to the summit in only 10 minutes.
  • ▶︎ Chickadee: Great for beginners and guests at the Cliff Lodge.
  • ▶︎ Wilbere: Explore the edge of the Gad Valley.
  • ▶︎ MidGad: Get off in the middle or ride to the top for lunch.
  • ▶︎ Baby Thunder: Access the perfect ski area for the whole family.
  • ▶︎ Gadzoom: Experience the best of the Gad Valley.
  • ▶︎ Mineral Basin Express: Explore the powder-filled basin on the other side of the summit.
  • ▶︎ Baldy: Ski from Snowbird to Alta and double the terrain.
  • ▶︎ Peruvian Express: Nearly to the top in one shot. Access to Mineral Basin through the tunnel.
  • ▶︎ Little Cloud: From Gad to the summit.
  • ▶︎ Gad 2: Access extreme terrain in the upper Gad Valley.
