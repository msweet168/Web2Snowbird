The Iron Blosam

The ski lodge with style.

The Iron Blosam lodge will give you a first class timeshare experience. Enjoy a swimming pool, indoor hot tub, fitness center, restaurant, and game room.

In the mood for Italian? Head over to The Wildflower for delicious pizzas, pastas, and various wines.

Staying connected is easy with the lodge's free wifi and getting to the mountain is quick and free with complimentary suttle service.

The Iron Blosam Lodge is the perfect place to have an unforgettable vacation.
