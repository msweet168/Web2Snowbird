The Lodge

The feel of home, right on the mountain.

With many variations of condomniums, The Lodge will give you all the comforts of home during your vacation. Choose a hotel bedroom or a one bedroom with a loft.

The lodge is only a short walk away from Snowbird Center where you can eat at delicious restaurants such as The Steak House and The Forklift or hop on the Ariel Tram for a quick ride up the mountain.
