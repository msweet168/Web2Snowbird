Dining

The best food at 11,000 feet.

The Aerie:

Enjoy delicious modern-American food high above the Cliff Lodge on floor 10. If you're a wine lover, the Arie is perfect for you. Browse the wine selecton using iPads to find the perfect glass for you. With beautiful mountain views and delicious food, your meal will be unforgettable. Serving Breakfast and Dinner daily.

El Chanate:

In the mood for Mexican? El Chanate has got you covered. Located on level A of The Cliff Lodge, stop by to experience the traditional Mexican flavor and atmosphere. Serving Lunch and Dinner daily.
